Emerging Technology Integration

Show the right piece
to the right person.

Our campaign uses AI-powered personalization to reach exactly the right eco-conscious buyers — at the right moment.

1

Scroll Instagram or click an ad

Meta Advantage+ AI detects users who follow eco-fashion, sustainable living, and handmade jewellery pages — and shows them our campaign Reel automatically.

2

Watch the transformation

A 30-second Reel shows the full journey — waste material collected, jewellery handcrafted, final piece worn. Real hands. Real story. No stock footage.

3

Personalized product suggestions

Based on engagement — earrings Reels vs necklace posts — the AI retargets users with the specific product category they showed interest in.

4

One tap to WhatsApp or shop

Every ad links directly to our WhatsApp catalogue or Instagram Shop. Choose your piece, customise colour or style, and order in minutes.

5

Share your piece, join the movement

Post-purchase, buyers are invited to share with #WearTheChange. The best posts are featured on our page — turning buyers into brand ambassadors.

📱 AI Personalization Technology

Powered by Meta Advantage+ & Instagram's Algorithm

Meta Advantage+ and Google PMax now use AI to automatically optimize ad creatives, targeting, and delivery — at zero extra cost beyond the ad budget.

This means our jewellery reaches exactly the right eco-conscious consumers, without expensive manual targeting setup.

Future upgrade: AR try-on filter on Instagram — let users virtually wear our jewellery before buying. Planned for Year 2.

Our Pledge

We market responsibly.
No exceptions.

No Greenwashing

Every eco-claim we make is backed by actual production practice. Our jewellery IS made from recycled waste — we never exaggerate or use vague sustainability terms without proof.

Full Influencer Transparency

All sponsored content is tagged #Ad or #Gifted. We brief every collaborator on ASCI disclosure guidelines before they post. No exceptions.

Honest Pricing Always

We don't use fake discounts or overstate affordability. Our pricing (₹150–₹900) is clearly communicated as value-for-craft. No manipulation.

Data Privacy (DPDP Act 2023)

Our WhatsApp broadcast uses only opt-in contacts. AI personalization runs through Meta's standard platform tools only — no unauthorized data collection, ever.
